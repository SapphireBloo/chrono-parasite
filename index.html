How to Play

Drain time, evolve, and outlast every other parasite in the arena.

Objective

Stay alive by stealing time from orbs and other parasites. More time = you age from Young → Adult → Elder, unlocking new abilities.

  • Young: Fast, only Tentacle.
  • Adult: Gains Dash.
  • Elder: Gains Shield / Parry.

Controls

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Primary – Tentacle (LMB): Aim with the mouse, click to latch and drain time.
  • Secondary – Dash (RMB, Adult+): Burst forward. On hit, steal a big chunk of time; on miss, lose time.
  • Shield / Parry (SPACE, Elder): Brief barrier. If an enemy hits you, they lose time and are slowed.

Tips

  • Eat orbs early to reach Adult quickly.
  • Use Tentacle at max range to stay safer.
  • Dash is high risk, high reward — use it to finish weakened enemies.
  • Elder shield can turn an enemy’s attack into your advantage.
